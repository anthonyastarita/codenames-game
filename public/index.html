Welcome to Codenames!

Codenames is a board-game where two teams compete by each having a Spymaster give one-word clues that can point to multiple words on the board. The other players on the team attempt to guess their team's words while avoiding the words of the other team.

Our bot will display a hint based on a random algorithm. Your job is to guess which words correspond to that hint.
